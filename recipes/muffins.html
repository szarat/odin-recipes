Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mashed bananas
  • 1/4 Cup creamy almond butter
  • 2 cups of old fashioned oats
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Combine all of the ingredients together in a mixing bowl with a rubber spatula.
  2. The cookie dough will be more wet since there is no flour. Itâ€™s slightly more difficult to work with. To help with this, refrigerate the dough for 15 minutes before forming into cookies.
  3. Scoop the dough with a medium sized cookie scoop. Place the dough in between your hands to squeeze it together and roll it to form a dough ball. Then, gently press to flatten. Place it on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Repeat until all the dough has been used. You can also just drop spoonfuls onto the cookie sheet and shape each spoonful into a cookie-like shape.
